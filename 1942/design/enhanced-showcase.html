Art Style Guidelines

  • Sharp, angular shapes with clear edges
  • Mathematical precision in patterns
  • Consistent geometric vocabulary
  • No organic/Ghibli-style elements
  • Functional beauty through geometry
  • Limited but effective color palettes
  • Performance-conscious implementation
  • Scalable across different tile sizes
